  • Maraetai, this is and excellent bike park with over 50km + of trails
    on offer for all skill levels.

  • Duders Park, this place does not have any technical or jump trails but is rather a
    simple loop around the regional park.

  • 440 Mtb park, this is arguably the best place to ride in the whole of Auckland
    as it provides a paid shuttle service to the peak for fast paced adrenaline
    filled rides to the bottom. Important note: not for beginners.
