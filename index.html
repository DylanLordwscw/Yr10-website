Trail Finder.

This is a list of every bike trail throughout auckland

South Auckland

South auckland is home to a good variety of trails however some are quite far away.

  • Puni park, this is a super fun trail network located down by pukekohe.

  • Ambury park has a good selection of loops, however there are no super
    challenging trails.

  • Hunua ranges, Hunua is a super fun place to ride but dont go here when
    its muddy as it is in dense bush.

East Auckland

2. The Eastern bays of auckland are home to a great variety of trails and parks.

  • Maraetai / Whitford This is and excellent bike park with over 50km + of trails
    on offer for all skill levels.

  • Duders Park, this place does not have any technical trails but is rather a
    simple loop around the park.

  • 440 Mtb park, this is arguably the best place to ride in the whole of auckland
    as it provides a shuttle service to the top for fast paced adrenaline
    filled rides to the bottom. Important note: not for beginners.

North Harbour

3. Across the bridge you will find quite a few trails including another contestant
for Aucklands best trail network, Woodhill.

  • Woodhill contains many many trails of all variety, all ontop of a fun dry sandy
    base that is rideable all year round.

  • Aswell as riverhead forest, this is not a area for beginenrs as its extremely
    easy to get lost and you will be sharing the park with dirt bikers.

  • Ontop of these two excellence places to ride there is birkenhead dirt jumps which
    have a good few jumps however there is a no dig no ride rule here,
    which unexperienced jumpers will have to learn about.

  • Sanders reserve, located just by paremoremo has many trails on offer and is a
    fun place to go for a few hours.

West Auckland

4. Over to the west there is one trail on offer, the beverage track.

  • The beverage track is a very easy cross country trail that starts just outside
    of sunnyvale.

Central Auckland

5. There isnt much here in the central city however there is Arch Hill, this is home
to a variety of tight windy cross country / tech trails.

  • On top of that a short ferry ride across the harbour can get you to waiheke island
    bike trails these have many, many, trails and you can spend a full
    day here without riding all the trails.
