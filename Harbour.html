North Harbour

3.   Across the bridge you will find quite a few trails including another contestant for Aucklands best trail network, Woodhill.

  • Woodhill contains many many trails of all variety, all ontop of a fun dry sandy
    base that is rideable all year round. It also has bikes for rent
    and trails for all skill levels.

  • Riverhead Forest, this is not a area for beginenrs as its extremely
    easy to get lost. Another note is that you will be sharing the park with dirt
    bikers.

  • Ontop of these two excellence places to ride there is birkenhead dirt jumps which
    have a good few jumps however there is no trail map or any facilities
    and the jumps are quite big which unexperienced jumpers will struggle on.

  • Sanders reserve, located just by paremoremo has many trails on offer and is a
    fun place to go for a few hours.
