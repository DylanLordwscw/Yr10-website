Central Auckland

5.   There isnt much here in the central city, as you would expect in a dense urban area.

  • Arch Hill, this is home to a variety of tight windy cross
    country / tech trails. These trails are fun if your looking for a post school / work ride.

  • Waiheke, a short ferry ride across the harbour can get you to Waiheke Island
    Waiheke has many, many, trails and you can spend a full
    day here without riding all the trails.
